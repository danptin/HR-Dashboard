# HR Analysis Dashboard | Power BI Project

## 📊 Project Overview

The HR Analytics Dashboard is an interactive Power BI project designed to provide workforce insights and support data-driven HR decision-making. The dashboard helps HR managers monitor employee demographics, compensation trends, workforce growth, and leave management through a centralized reporting solution.


# 🎯 Business Requirements

The dashboard was developed to answer the following business questions:

- How many people are in each job?
- What is the gender breakdown of the staff?
- What is the age spread of the staff?
- Which jobs pay more?
- Who are the top earners in each job?
- What is the relationship between qualification and salary?
- What is the staff growth trend over time?
- Can employees be filtered by the starting letter of their name?
- How are employee leave balances distributed?
- How can HR managers quickly monitor key workforce KPIs?

# 💡 Key Insights
- Female employees account for approximately 55% of the workforce.
- Most employees fall within the 25–35 age group.
- Packaging Associate has the highest employee count.
- Employees with Master's degrees generally receive higher salaries.
- The organization has shown consistent workforce growth over time.
